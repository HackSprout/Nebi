About

At Nebi, we believe that the internet should be an open universe for everyone to explore.
Our mission is to build tools that empower users of all abilities to navigate the web effortlessly and independently.
With gaze tracking and voice commands, Nebi transforms the way you interact with the digital world ~ making exploration faster, more intuitive, and more accessible than ever before.
Whether you're browsing, learning, shopping, or creating, Nebi helps you move through the internet with the same freedom and curiosity you'd bring to exploring the stars.
Join us in our journey to explore the vast universe of the web.
